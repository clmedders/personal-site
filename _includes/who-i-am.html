What Wakes Me Up In The Morning

I work on the Testing and QA side of the development with the goal to become a badass developer.

However, I am much more than that... I am also a son, brother, friend, husband, and a dad. Born and raised in Aiken, SC I met the love of my life in college and have grown an awesome life. We have one child and with all the craziness that will bring, I really can't imagine it being any other way. I am so grateful that you are here and hope that when you leave you to have a better idea of who I am, what I can do, and maybe have a desire to connect.
